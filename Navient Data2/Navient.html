California Home to Majority of Country's Navient Complaints

By: Ben Strober


Since 2017, Navient has been under an immense amount of pressure due to lawsuits and complaints. From 2020-2023, the nation's highest populated states dominated the amount of complaints.






Florida, Texas and California, are the U.S's three highest populated states, so it's no surprise they dominante the amount of complaints. Over the last three years, California had the most in the country with 513 complaints. Florida had 458 and Texas had 408.

Overall, Florida, California and Texas accounted for 24.26% of all complaints in the United States since 2020.
